Privacy Policy – MCAAB

Last updated: March 2026

Missed Call As A Business (MCAAB) provides a service that sends a transactional SMS to customers who attempt to call a business but the call is missed or unanswered.

Information We Collect

  • Caller phone number
  • Call time and duration
  • Call status (missed, busy, answered)
  • SMS delivery status

How We Use Information

We use this information to deliver transactional SMS notifications, operate the platform, and improve system reliability.

SMS and Phone Number Usage

Phone numbers collected through our system are used only to deliver transactional notifications related to missed calls and ordering links.

We do not sell or share phone numbers with third parties for marketing purposes.
