Service Description

Missed Call As A Business (MCAAB) allows businesses to send a transactional SMS message to customers who attempt to call but the call is missed or unanswered.

Message Frequency

Customers typically receive one message per missed call attempt.

Message and Data Rates

Message and data rates may apply depending on the customer's mobile carrier plan.

Opt-Out

Customers can reply STOP to unsubscribe from messages.

Help

Customers can reply HELP for assistance.
